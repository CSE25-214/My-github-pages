The Story
Built around the bottle

Copper & Oak started as a single barrel of bourbon and a folding table. Today it's a full liquor bar and kitchen, but the idea hasn't changed: pour generously, cook simply, and let people stay as long as they like.

Our back bar holds over 200 spirits, from dusty single barrels to the bottle you had at your grandfather's table. The kitchen runs small and seasonal, built to sit alongside a drink rather than compete with it.
