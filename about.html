Our Story

Rooted in Bobonong

GLADEATS opened as a gathering place for Bobonong — somewhere friends could share a cold drink or a Scotch and water over a proper plate of seswaa. We wanted a spot that felt as warm as the village itself.

Our bar keeps local favourites and international spirits side by side, while our kitchen leans on recipes passed down through Central District households — seswaa, mogodu and braai, cooked over open flame and served without fuss.

Every drink we sell is licensed and regulated under Botswana's liquor laws, and we take responsible service seriously — good times, done right.
