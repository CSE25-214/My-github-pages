Woven Into the Village
Our Story
Rooted in Bobonong

GLADEATS opened its doors as a gathering place for Bobonong — somewhere friends could share a cold Chibuku or a Scotch and water over a proper plate of seswaa. We wanted a spot that felt as warm as the village itself.

Our bar keeps local favourites and international spirits side by side, while the kitchen leans on recipes passed down through Central District households, cooked over open flame and served without fuss.
